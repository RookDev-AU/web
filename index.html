🍳 Frugal Kitchen — Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Frugal Kitchen ("the App") is a recipe and pantry management application. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Information you provide:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your display name and email address for account identification.
  • Pantry and shopping data: Items you add to your pantry and shopping list.
  • Recipe data: Recipes you generate, save to favourites, or add to your meal plan.
  • Photos: Images you take to scan food items or submit as recipe photos.
  • API keys: If you choose the "Bring Your Own Key" plan, your Gemini API key is stored locally on your device only.

Information we do NOT collect:

  • Location data
  • Contacts
  • Financial information (payments handled by Google Play)
  • Health data
  • Device identifiers or advertising IDs

How We Use Your Information

  • Food identification: Photos are sent to Google's Gemini AI to identify food items. Images are processed in real-time and not stored after identification.
  • Recipe generation: Your pantry items are sent to Google's Gemini AI to generate recipe suggestions.
  • Data sync: Your pantry, shopping list, recipes, and favourites sync via Google Firebase for cross-device and family sharing access.
  • Recipe photos: Submitted recipe photos are stored in Firebase Storage and may be visible to other users after approval.

Data Storage and Security

  • App data is stored on Google Firebase servers with industry-standard security.
  • Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS).
  • We do not sell, trade, or transfer your personal information to third parties.

Family Sharing

If you create or join a household, your pantry and shopping list data is shared with household members. You can leave a household at any time.

Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase — authentication, database, storage
  • Google Gemini AI — food identification, recipe generation
  • Open Food Facts — barcode scanning
  • TheMealDB — recipe images

Children's Privacy

Frugal Kitchen is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Deletion

You can delete your data by:

  • Clearing your pantry, shopping list, and favourites within the app
  • Leaving any household you've joined
  • Signing out of the app
  • Contacting us at frugalkitchen.app@gmail.com for full data deletion

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.

Contact Us
